# Flight Booking
**Type:** Concept
**Related:** [Shin Travels](../entities/shin-travels.md)
**Source:** [Company Website](../sources/company-website.md)
## Description
Flight Booking is a core service offered by Shin Travels that enables clients to reserve air travel across international and domestic routes. The service includes itinerary planning, fare comparison, and booking confirmation with support for multiple airlines and travel classes.
## Key Details
- Bookings are processed through verified airline partners
- Includes itinerary planning and fare comparison
- Supports multiple travel classes and domestic/international routes
