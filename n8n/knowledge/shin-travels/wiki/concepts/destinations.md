# Destinations

## Pakistan
- **Lahore** — Cultural heart: Badshahi Mosque, Food Street, Wagah Border ceremony. From £379-429 pp one-way
- **Islamabad** — Serene capital: Faisal Mosque, Margalla Hills, Daman-e-Koh viewpoint. From £449-455 pp
- **Karachi** — Coastal giant: Clifton Beach, Quaid's Mausoleum, Burns Road street food. From £449 pp
- **Hunza Valley** — Northern paradise: breathtaking mountain scenery, ancient Baltit Fort
- **Flights from UK:** Direct from London Heathrow, Manchester, Birmingham (7-9 hours)
- **Airlines:** Emirates, Qatar Airways, Saudia, PIA, Virgin Atlantic
- **Best months:** October to March for pleasant weather
- **Food:** Lahori chargha, seekh kebabs, nihari, karak chai, gulab jamun
- **Tips:** Carry Pakistani rupees, use Careem/Bykea, buy local SIM at airport, dress modestly

## India
- **Delhi** — History and spice: Red Fort, Chandni Chowk. From £380-385 pp one-way
- **Mumbai** — Bollywood and business: colonial architecture, bustling markets. From £399 pp

## UAE — Dubai
- **From £479-546 pp one-way**
- Burj Khalifa, Burj Al Arab (7-star hotel), Atlantis The Palm
- Luxury shopping, Michelin-starred dining (Nobu, Zuma)
- Private desert safaris with dining under the stars
- Helicopter transfers and private yacht charters available

## UAE — Abu Dhabi
- **From £479 pp**
- Louvre Abu Dhabi, cultural attractions
- Anantara Al Baleen — resort living on water with private villas

## Saudi Arabia
- **Jeddah** — Gateway to Makkah, Red Sea coast. From £449-629 pp
- **Makkah** — Holiest city, Umrah/Hajj destination. Hotels near Haram
- **Madinah** — Prophet's Mosque. From £549 pp
- **Riyadh** — Modern capital. From £1,099 pp
- **Red Sea Project** — New luxury destination with private island resorts, pristine coral reefs

## Qatar — Doha
- **From £546-549 pp**
- National Museum of Qatar, Mathaf: Arab Museum of Modern Art
- Souq Waqif, Katara Hills, Banana Island Resort
- More refined, cultured alternative to Dubai

## Japan
- **Tokyo** — Shibuya Crossing, Meiji Shrine, Akihabara, Toyosu Market, Shinjuku izakayas
- **Kyoto** — Kinkaku-ji, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Gion district, tea ceremonies
- **Osaka** — Dotonbori district, takoyaki, okonomiyaki
- **Hakone** — Natural onsen with Mount Fuji views
- **Hiroshima** — Peace Memorial Park, Miyajima Island
- **Nara** — Sacred deer, Todai-ji Great Buddha
- **Tips:** Japan Rail Pass saves hundreds, rent pocket WiFi, carry cash, best in spring (Mar-May) or autumn (Oct-Nov)

## USA
- **West Coast:** Los Angeles, Pacific Coast Highway, San Francisco, Yosemite, Big Sur
- **Southwest:** Grand Canyon, Zion, Bryce Canyon, Monument Valley, Antelope Canyon
- **East Coast:** Great Smoky Mountains, Nashville, New York City
- **Tips:** Book rental cars early, pack layers, download offline maps, carry sun protection

## Oman
- **Muscat** — Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque, Muttrah Souq, Qantab Beach
- **Wahiba Sands** — Desert camp, dune buggies, stargazing
- **Musandam Peninsula** — "Norway of Arabia": dhow cruises, dolphins, sea turtles, snorkeling
- Over 500 ancient forts and castles
- More authentic, affordable alternative to Dubai
- Easy to combine with UAE stopover
