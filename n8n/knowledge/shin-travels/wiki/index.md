## Overview

SHIN Travels was founded in 1998 by a family with a conviction that every traveller deserves the same level of care and expertise regardless of budget. What began as a modest travel consultancy in East London has grown into one of the UK's most trusted independent travel agencies, serving thousands of clients across the country.
